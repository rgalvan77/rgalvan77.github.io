"Me Saying Goodbye Until Later Comes: Rodney's Bookstore in Central Square"

Boston Hassle, September 21, 2020

Rodney’s Bookstore will shut down its shop in Cambridge’s Central Square at the end of October, this year 2020. It intends to later reopen elsewhere which I reckon will indeed happen, but who’s to say when and where, in this city of gentrifying development and storefronts?
